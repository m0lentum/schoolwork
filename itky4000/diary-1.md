# Mistä tulen — mihin haluan?

Arst

\begin{equation} \label{Eq_NS_momentum}
  \frac{\partial \mathbf{u}}{\partial t} =
    -(\mathbf{u} \cdot \nabla) \mathbf{u}
    - \frac{1}{\mathbf{\rho}} \nabla p
    + \nu \nabla^2 \mathbf{u}
    + \mathbf{F}_{ext},
\end{equation}

\begin{equation} \label{Eq_NS_incompr}
  \nabla \cdot \mathbf{u} = 0.
\end{equation}
